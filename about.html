Our mission

To provide evidence-based expertise and high-quality research that strengthens international collaboration and solves the demanding technological constraints.

Our founding

Established in 1996, our organization was built on a foundation of rigorous academic research and practical industrial application.
We maintain long-standing affiliation with National Technical University "Kharkiv Polytechnic Institute", and cooperate with Ukraine's industrial enterprises, including: Motor Sich, Zaporizhstal, Zaporizhzhia Metallurgical Plant, Malyshev Plant, etc.
With three decades of cumulative experience, we bring a solid perspective to every project.
